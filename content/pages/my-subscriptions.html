My Subscriptions

Bug Alert notices can be delivered to you by email, phone call, or SMS.

Before you can manage your notice subscriptions, please verify your email address. After you've submitted, you can close this tab and head over to your email inbox.

Only vulnerabilities that Bug Alert has rated as 'critical severity' will trigger a phone call, and notices of 'high severity' or 'very high severity' issues will not.
More information about the rating system can be found on our GitHub documentation.
