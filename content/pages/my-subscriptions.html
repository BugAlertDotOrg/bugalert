Bug Alert notices can be delivered to you by email, phone call, or SMS.

Before you can manage your notice subscriptions, please verify your email address. After you've submitted, you can close this tab and head over to your email inbox.

Only vulnerabilities that Bug Alert has rated as 'very high severity' or 'critical severity' will trigger a phone call, and notices of 'high severity' issues will not.
More information about the rating system can be found on our GitHub documentation.

A quick note for our friends outside of the United States and Canada.

Our currently-selected telephony provider can only serve phone numbers in the US or Canada. Obviously, this isn't super ideal, but after we've gotten this service launched and more widely adopted, we will circle back and at least see if we can use a service like Twillio to provide, at a minimum, SMS support for global phone numbers. For now, supply your phone number if you are interested in eventually getting phone calls or SMS messages, and we'll keep in touch with updates.

Until we have better international phone support, this page will strongly recommend also opting into email notifications if subscribing to phone calls or SMS.
